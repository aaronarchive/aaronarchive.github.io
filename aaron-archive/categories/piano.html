Drift: Gale of November

My 5th composition, 2023. Originally composed around 2021.

I was inspired by the chilly wind of November and the sight of dragonflies at dusk back in Southeast Asia. The tranquil atmosphere deeply relaxed me, prompting me to capture the moment by turning to my piano. I composed a piece to preserve this beautiful memory. I would love to hear your thoughts on it.
