Angel on Earth Aug Hope Nature

You are an angel
Who is on earth
You may not know that
As you are lost with thoughts

You do have wings
Yet you do not fly
For your mind
Take you to place
Where I may not be able to find

Your thoughts hide you
In the cave or ocean
Sometime the muddy ground
Sometime you set yourself in motion

Sometime you fly
With your imaginary wings
And so, for awhile
To be in your dream
But with the fear to fall you wouldn’t jump

For you lives like a mere human
With fear and love
Your lips so sweet
When you started talk
No mere human would survive
As they built with fault

You remove the darkness
And you became the light.
You embrace the darkness
And live a lonesome life

Around this judging people
You set yourself free
You care the animals
So, do plant and trees

You seek freedom
From the nature beauty
As it is the only one
Which give you tranquility.

For the way you are
Some people may judge
Some say you are an idiot.
Some say you are the worse.

But I like the way
You know your worth
So, I say you are an angel
Who is on earth.

Guru Poornima July Reflective Nature

I am a mere human.
With insecurity and fear
During the hour of darkness
Seeking light
My Guru reminds me
To see the sun
The crimson ray
Touching the sky
Oh, Surya Deva!
How I am blessed to see
The darkness is overthrown.
By the light of thee

Some of the sadness I swallow
Stop the tears from falling from my eyes.
My Guru told me
"There Indra Deva"
The rain has been blessed.
So, I can cry.
Without showing my tears

With a heavy heart
I took a walk.
My Guru said
to felt the gale of wind.
To ease my burden,
How grateful I am
Vayu Deva,

These beautiful flowers
That thrives on the planet
The trees, plants, and animals
Each time I am drowned in sorrow,
When I lost hope
See no tomorrow.
My Guru told me
To seek beauty
From the forest, trees, plants, and afar
What a blessing from Dharti Maa.

Oh! Not to forget my guru who taught me
To value the astounding beauty of these
The lesson still remains, even though you are away.
To you, I humbly bow down to this day.

A tribute to my late grandfather.
